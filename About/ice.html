Cece Answers Your Ice Breaker Questions

How much does a polar bear weigh?

What is your dream job?

I think it would be awesome to work in UX. I read a book that described UX as curiosity meets passion meets empathy. I really self identify with those three traits.

I really like web development. I think it would be nice to work on enterprise web applications in a corporate setting. I want to master MVC architecture and become great at working with databases.

If you only had 45 min. to prototype a robot dating web site describe what you'd build.

I think the color scheme would be white, and salmon with dark reds menus and robot grey text. I give each robot profile a "download my brain" button. So the robot users could fork the operating system of prospective partners and take them for a test drive. There would also be the capability to send binary love letters.

What is the greatest compliment that you can give or receive?

This place is better because you are here.

I love people who make the environment around them better. I strive to do the same.
